How It Works
  1. Click "Start Camera" to activate webcam
  2. Hold staff ID card in front of camera to scan QR code
  3. Position staff member's face in camera for verification
  4. Face auto-captures after 2 seconds (or click "Capture Face")
  5. Attendance is marked only once per day per staff member
Tip: Enable "Continuous Mode" for unattended operation. System will auto-restart after each scan. Add ?autostart=true to URL for auto-start on page load.
